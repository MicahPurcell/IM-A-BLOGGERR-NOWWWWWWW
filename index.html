PLACES YOU MUST VISIT

HOG ISLAND GRENADA

Visited by me on 06/11/2021

Hog island is filled with warm and friendly people whilst surrounded in nature and vegetation. Enjoy every moment is an easy thing to do, just let yourself be. From climate waters to hot weather hog island is just the place to be.

WELCOME STONE GRENADA

Visited by me on 13/04/2018

Welcome stone, what a lovely view of my home island Grenada.From our great lake Grand Etang to our Forest Reserve welcome rock really gives you a birds eye view.Looking over the sea, you feel free and that's how you know it's the place to be.

Movies you must watch

AVENGERES ENDGAME
Amazing movie for family and friends, definatly recommend

Adrift in space with no food or water, Tony Stark sends a message to Pepper Potts as his oxygen supply starts to dwindle. Meanwhile, the remaining Avengers -- Thor, Black Widow, Captain America and Bruce Banner -- must figure out a way to bring back their vanquished allies for an epic showdown with Thanos -- the evil demigod who decimated the planet and the universe.

LION KING
Enjoy watch Simba grow from a young cub to a true lion.

Simba idolizes his father, King Mufasa, and takes to heart his own royal destiny on the plains of Africa. But not everyone in the kingdom celebrates the new cub's arrival. Scar, Mufasa's brother -- and former heir to the throne -- has plans of his own. The battle for Pride Rock is soon ravaged with betrayal, tragedy and drama, ultimately resulting in Simba's exile. Now, with help from a curious pair of newfound friends, Simba must figure out how to grow up and take back what is rightfully his.

MOANA
Enjoy Moana find the meaning and life of her people.

An adventurous teenager sails out on a daring mission to save her people. During her journey, Moana meets the once-mighty demigod Maui, who guides her in her quest to become a master way-finder. Together they sail across the open ocean on an action-packed voyage, encountering enormous monsters and impossible odds. Along the way, Moana fulfills the ancient quest of her ancestors and discovers the one thing she always sought: her own identity.
